Spicy Beef Rice

Introduction

This is a family recipe only recently invented by my brother and thus passed unto me. It is very much a bachelor meal in its presentation and simplicity, but at the same time a comfort food. It is also the perfect dish to pack into leftovers for lunch.

Ingredients

1lb Ground Beef
Chili Paste
Rice
Various Veggies
  • Corn
  • Peas
  • Soy Beans
  • Green Beans
Mushrooms
Cooking Oil
  • Corn Oil
  • Canola Oil
  • Olive Oil
Parsley

Procedure (Click steps to hide them. Click procedure to reset!)

  1. Oil pan and heat on medium
  2. Pan fry beef in pan on medium heat
  3. Add mushrooms to pan and cook until tender
  4. Add vegetables of choice and cook until tender
  5. Drain oil
  6. Season with chili paste and any other chosen spices
  7. Add rice and mix thoroughly. Turn heat to low
  8. Garnish with sprig of parsley

Conclusion

It certainly is not the prettiest of presentations for a meal, but this is a solid staple for dinners and lunches. The preparation and cooking time is minimal, and ingredients are common-place in almost any store. Recommended for those short on time but high on hunger!
